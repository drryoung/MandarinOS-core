# MandarinOS Orientation Vocabulary Pack (v1)

Purpose: Support the Place and Travel engines with simple vocabulary for
distance and transport.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Distance Questions

离这儿远吗？\
lí zhèr yuǎn ma\
Is it far from here?

要多久？\
yào duōjiǔ\
How long does it take?

怎么去？\
zěnme qù\
How do you get there?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Transport Methods

坐火车\
zuò huǒchē\
take the train

坐飞机\
zuò fēijī\
take a plane

开车\
kāichē\
drive

走路\
zǒulù\
walk

坐地铁\
zuò dìtiě\
take the subway

坐公交车\
zuò gōngjiāo chē\
take the bus

------------------------------------------------------------------------
