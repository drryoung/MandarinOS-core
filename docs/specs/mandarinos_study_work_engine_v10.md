
# MandarinOS Conversation Engine
# Study / Work Engine v10

Purpose:
Discuss what someone studies or what work they do, including university background,
job nature, industry, salary, entrepreneurship, freelancing, and future of work.

ENTRY QUESTION

你是做什么的？
What do you do?

Examples:
我是学生 – I am a student
我是工程师 – I am an engineer
我是医生 – I am a doctor
我做生意 – I do business

STUDENT BRANCH

你学什么？ – What do you study?
你的学校在哪儿？ – Where is your school?
你几年级？ – What year are you in?

UNIVERSITY BRANCH

你在哪儿上的大学？ – Where did you go to university?
这是985大学吗？ – Is it a 985 university?
还是211大学？ – Or a 211 university?
这个大学最有名的是什么？ – What is it famous for?
你学什么专业？ – What is your major?

FUTURE CAREER

你毕业以后想做什么？ – What do you want to do after graduation?
你想进入什么行业？ – What industry?
现在找工作容易吗？ – Is it easy to find work now?

WORK BRANCH

你做什么工作？ – What job do you do?
你在哪个公司工作？ – Which company?
你的公司在哪个城市？ – Which city?

COMPANY

你的公司大吗？ – Is the company big?
你们公司有多少人？ – How many employees?
这是国企吗？ – State company?
这是外企吗？ – Foreign company?
还是中国公司？ – Chinese company?

JOB NATURE

你主要做什么？ – What do you mainly do?
你平时一天主要做什么？ – What do you do daily?
你的工作主要负责什么？ – What are you responsible for?
这个工作难吗？ – Is the job difficult?
为什么？ – Why?
你做这个工作多久了？ – How long have you done it?

INDUSTRY

你是做什么行业的？ – What industry are you in?

INDUSTRY QUESTIONS

这个行业现在发展得好吗？ – Is the industry growing?
这个行业竞争大吗？ – Is competition strong?
这个行业容易进入吗？ – Is it easy to enter?
你是怎么进入这个行业的？ – How did you enter it?

WORK PRESSURE

你现在工作压力大吗？ – Is work pressure high?
越来越大吗？ – Is it getting worse?
这个行业现在卷吗？ – Is the industry very competitive?

SALARY

这个行业工资高吗？ – Are salaries high?
刚开始工资大概多少？ – Starting salary roughly?

FREELANCE / SIDE BUSINESS

你是自己做还是在公司上班？ – Self-employed or company?
你是做自由职业吗？ – Are you freelancing?
你怎么找到客户？ – How do you find clients?
你有副业吗？ – Do you have a side business?

ENTREPRENEURSHIP

你想自己创业吗？ – Do you want to start a business?
你想做什么生意？ – What business?
你觉得创业难吗？ – Is entrepreneurship difficult?

FUTURE OF INDUSTRY

你觉得这个行业以后会怎么样？ – What will happen to the industry?
AI会影响这个行业吗？ – Will AI affect it?
