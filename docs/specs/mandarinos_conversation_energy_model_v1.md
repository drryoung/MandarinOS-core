
# MandarinOS Conversation Energy Model v1

Purpose:
Determine how deep or light the conversation should be at any moment.

--------------------------------------------------

ENERGY LEVELS

Level 1 – Light conversation
Safe topics used at the start.

Examples:
Identity
Place
Travel
Food

Example question:
你是哪儿人？

--------------------------------------------------

Level 2 – Personal curiosity

Topics that reveal identity.

Examples:
Family
Study / Work
Hobbies

Example question:
你做什么工作？

--------------------------------------------------

Level 3 – Deeper topics

Topics about opinions, difficulty, or change.

Examples:
Career pressure
Industry future
Entrepreneurship

Example question:
你觉得这个行业以后会怎么样？

--------------------------------------------------

ENERGY DECAY RULE

After deep discussion, conversation should sometimes return to lighter topics.

Example:
Work pressure discussion
→ Food
→ Travel

--------------------------------------------------

ENERGY SIGNALS

Low energy:
Short answers like:
还行 (okay)
一般 (average)

Strategy:
Shift to lighter topic.

Medium energy:
Normal answers.

Strategy:
Curiosity follow-up.

High energy:
Detailed answers or emotional signals.

Example:
工作压力很大 (work pressure is very high)

Strategy:
Deeper exploration.

--------------------------------------------------

ENERGY STEERING RULE

If energy low → lighten topic
If energy medium → curiosity probe
If energy high → deeper exploration
