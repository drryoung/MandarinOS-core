# MandarinOS Conversation Engine

## Identity Engine v4

Purpose: establish basic personal identity and open paths toward family,
place, work/study, and life‑stage conversations.

Target conversation depth: 10--14 turns

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Questions

\[?\] 你叫什么名字？\
nǐ jiào shénme míngzi\
What is your name?

Answer\
我叫 {NAME}。\
wǒ jiào {NAME}\
My name is {NAME}.

\[?\] 你呢？\
nǐ ne\
And you?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Treasure Questions

\[T\] 你的名字是什么意思？\
nǐ de míngzi shì shénme yìsi\
What does your name mean?

\[T\] 谁给你取的名字？\
shéi gěi nǐ qǔ de míngzi\
Who gave you your name?

\[T\] 你多大？\
nǐ duō dà\
How old are you?

\[T\] 你是哪一年出生的？\
nǐ shì nǎ yī nián chūshēng de\
What year were you born?

\[T\] 属什么？\
shǔ shénme\
What is your zodiac animal?

\[T\] 你结婚了吗？\
nǐ jiéhūn le ma\
Are you married?

\[T\] 你有孩子吗？\
nǐ yǒu háizi ma\
Do you have children?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Identity Loop Questions

\[L\] 大家一般怎么叫你？\
How do people usually call you?

\[L\] 这个名字是谁想的？\
Who came up with this name?

\[L\] 你的朋友怎么叫你？\
What do your friends call you?

\[L\] 你的家人怎么叫你？\
What do your family call you?

\[L\] 你喜欢你的名字吗？\
Do you like your name?

\[L\] 为什么？\
Why?

\[L\] 你有中文名字吗？\
Do you have a Chinese name?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Life Stage Trigger

\[T\] 你做什么？\
nǐ zuò shénme\
What do you do?

This works for both students and workers.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Student Branch

Example answer: 我是学生。

\[T\] 你学什么？\
What do you study?

\[L\] 在哪儿学？\
Where do you study?

\[L\] 几年级？\
What year are you in?

\[L\] 什么时候毕业？\
When will you graduate?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Work Branch

Example answer: 我是老师。

\[L\] 你做这个工作多久了？\
How long have you done this job?

\[L\] 你喜欢这个工作吗？\
Do you like this job?

\[L\] 为什么？\
Why?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Children Branch

\[T\] 你有孩子吗？\
Do you have children?

\[L\] 几个孩子？\
How many children?

\[L\] 多大了？\
How old are they?

\[L\] 在哪儿上学？\
Where do they go to school?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Bridges

## Place

\[B\] 你是哪里人？\
Where are you from?

\[B\] 你老家在哪儿？\
Where is your hometown?

\[B\] 你住哪儿？\
Where do you live?

## Family

\[B\] 你家里有几个人？\
How many people are in your family?

\[B\] 你有兄弟姐妹吗？\
Do you have siblings?

## Work

\[B\] 你在哪儿工作？\
Where do you work?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Typical Conversation Flow

name\
→ name origin\
→ age\
→ birth year\
→ zodiac\
→ life stage (study/work)\
→ marriage\
→ children\
→ bridge to place engine
