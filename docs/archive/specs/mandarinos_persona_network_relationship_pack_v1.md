# MandarinOS Persona Network & Relationship Vocabulary Pack v1

Purpose: Preserve the conceptual design for connected personas and the
relationship vocabulary that supports family conversations and persona
bridges.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Persona Network Concept

Instead of isolated conversation partners, MandarinOS uses a small
social network of personas so conversations can reference family
members, friends, and colleagues.

Example structure:

Li Jianguo (retired teacher) │ Wang Wei (engineer, son) │ Zhang Rui
(student, daughter) │ Liu Fang (accountant, cousin) │ Chen Hao
(designer, friend)

This allows natural conversational bridges such as:

你有孩子吗？ → 有一个女儿。

她叫什么名字？ → 张瑞。

Later the learner may talk to Zhang Rui. This makes MandarinOS feel like
a small social world.

# 3. Relationship Vocabulary Pack (P1)

## Core family

爸爸 / 父亲 --- father\
妈妈 / 母亲 --- mother\
儿子 --- son\
女儿 --- daughter

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Siblings

哥哥 --- older brother\
弟弟 --- younger brother\
姐姐 --- older sister\
妹妹 --- younger sister

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Marriage

丈夫 / 老公 --- husband\
妻子 / 老婆 --- wife

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Grandparents

爷爷 --- grandfather\
奶奶 --- grandmother

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Social Relationships

朋友 --- friend\
同学 --- classmate\
同事 --- colleague\
老师 --- teacher\
学生 --- student\
老板 --- boss

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Core Family Questions

你家有几个人？ --- How many people are in your family?\
你有兄弟姐妹吗？ --- Do you have siblings?\
你有孩子吗？ --- Do you have children?\
你爸爸做什么？ --- What does your father do?\
你妈妈在哪儿？ --- Where is your mother?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Loop Questions

他们在哪儿？ --- Where are they?\
他们做什么？ --- What do they do?\
你常见他们吗？ --- Do you see them often?\
他们喜欢什么？ --- What do they like?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Bridge Example

你妹妹做什么？ → 她是学生

This can naturally bridge to the student persona conversation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Design Principles

1.  Persona network should remain small (5--7 personas).
2.  Information about people should emerge gradually during
    conversation.
3.  Relationships enable natural conversation bridges.
4.  Focus on relationships people actually remember.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Future Expansion

Later versions may include:

-   deeper family trees
-   shared friend groups
-   travel relationships
-   work relationships

But v1 should remain simple and memorable.
