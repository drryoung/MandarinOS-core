
# MandarinOS Family Vocabulary Pack (P1)

Purpose: Provide extremely simple family vocabulary so true beginners can follow the Family conversation engine.

---

# Example Simple Sentences

我有一个哥哥。  
I have an older brother.

我没有兄弟姐妹。  
I don’t have siblings.

我有两个孩子。  
I have two children.

我的父母住在上海。  
My parents live in Shanghai.
