
# MandarinOS Travel Engine v4 (Locked Draft)

Purpose:
Discuss travel experiences, destinations, preferences, stories, and future travel plans.
Travel conversations tend to be story-driven and naturally engaging.

--------------------------------------------------
ENTRY PATH 1 — Direct Entry (P1)

你去过哪里？
Where have you been?

Example answers:
我去过北京 — I have been to Beijing
我去过上海 — I have been to Shanghai
我去过日本 — I have been to Japan

--------------------------------------------------
ENTRY PATH 2 — Place Engine Bridge

Example:
我是成都人 — I am from Chengdu

Bridge question:
你去过成都吗？
Have you been to Chengdu?

If yes:
什么时候去的？
When did you go?

If no:
你想去吗？
Do you want to go?

--------------------------------------------------
P1 TRAVEL LADDER

Destination
你去过哪里？
Where have you been?

Time
什么时候去的？
When did you go?

Travel companions
你跟谁去的？
Who did you go with?

Examples:
朋友 — friends
家人 — family
同事 — colleagues

Bridge → Family Engine

Travel purpose
你去旅游还是出差？
Did you go for travel or business?

旅游 — tourism
出差 — business trip

Bridge → Study/Work Engine

--------------------------------------------------
FIRST REACTION

那个地方怎么样？
How is that place?

--------------------------------------------------
CURIOSITY QUESTIONS (P1)

好玩吗？
Is it fun?

人多吗？
Are there many people?

远吗？
Is it far?

贵吗？
Is it expensive?

--------------------------------------------------
TRANSPORTATION BRANCH

你怎么去的？
How did you get there?

Examples:
飞机 — airplane
火车 — train
开车 — car

--------------------------------------------------
FOOD BRIDGE

那里的菜怎么样？
How is the food there?

有什么好吃的？
What good food is there?

Bridge → Food Engine

--------------------------------------------------
TRAVEL PREFERENCES

你喜欢城市还是自然？
Do you prefer cities or nature?

你喜欢海边还是山？
Do you prefer the seaside or mountains?

--------------------------------------------------
FUTURE TRAVEL

你想去哪里？
Where do you want to go?

为什么？
Why?

你最想去哪个国家？
Which country do you most want to visit?

--------------------------------------------------
BEST TRAVEL EXPERIENCE

你去过的地方，哪个最好？
Of the places you've visited, which is the best?

为什么最好？
Why is it the best?

你还想再去吗？
Would you go again?

--------------------------------------------------
WORST TRAVEL EXPERIENCE

有没有不太好的旅行？
Have you had a bad travel experience?

发生了什么？
What happened?

Examples:
天气不好 — bad weather
很多人 — too crowded
很贵 — very expensive

--------------------------------------------------
RECOMMENDATION BRANCH

你推荐吗？
Do you recommend it?

为什么推荐？
Why do you recommend it?

你推荐什么地方？
Which place do you recommend?

你推荐什么餐厅？
Which restaurant do you recommend?

Bridge → Food Engine

--------------------------------------------------
CULTURE BRANCH

那里的文化怎么样？
How is the culture there?

和中国一样吗？
Is it similar to China?

--------------------------------------------------
TRAVEL STORY BRANCH

有什么有趣的事情吗？
Did anything interesting happen?

有什么特别的事情吗？
Did anything special happen?

--------------------------------------------------
