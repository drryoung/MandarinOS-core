# MandarinOS Conversation Ladder Draft
Design owner: Raymond
Purpose: refine treasure questions and loop questions for richer conversations.


=====================================================
ENGINE: identity
=====================================================

CURRENT FRAMES
- (P1) 你好！ — nǐ hǎo!
- (P1) 你叫什么名字？ — nǐ jiào shénme míngzi?
- (P1) 我叫{NAME}。 — wǒ jiào {NAME}.
- (P1) 你呢？ — nǐ ne?
- (P1) 谢谢！ — xièxie!
- (P1) 不客气。 — bú kèqi.
- (P2) 我的名字有意思，因为{REASON_POS}。 — wǒ de míngzi yǒu yìsi, yīnwèi {REASON_POS}.
- (P2) 大家一般怎么叫你？ — dàjiā yībān zěnme jiào nǐ?
- (P2) 我平时比较{STYLE}。 — wǒ píngshí bǐjiào {STYLE}.
- (P2) 你觉得你的名字怎么样？ — nǐ juéde nǐ de míngzi zěnmeyàng?
- (P2) 这个名字对你有什么意义？ — zhège míngzi duì nǐ yǒu shénme yìyì?

TREASURE QUESTIONS (suggested)
- 你的名字是什么意思？ — What does your name mean?
- 谁给你取的名字？ — Who gave you your name?
- 你有中文名字吗？ — Do you have a Chinese name?

OXYGEN LOOP QUESTIONS
- 为什么？ — Why?
- 谁？ — Who?
- 什么时候？ — When?
- 哪里？ — Where?
- 怎么样？ — How is it?
- 喜欢吗？ — Do you like it?
- 跟谁一起？ — With whom?
- 什么时候开始？ — When did it start?

BRIDGE QUESTIONS
- 你是哪里人？ — Where are you from?
- 你现在住哪里？ — Where do you live now?
- 你做什么工作？ — What is your job?
- 你平常喜欢做什么？ — What do you usually like to do?
- 你最近忙吗？ — Are you busy recently?

=====================================================
UNIVERSAL CONVERSATION LOOP QUESTIONS (High Leverage)
=====================================================

These questions can extend almost any conversation topic and are designed
to keep dialogue flowing even with simple vocabulary.

- 为什么？ — Why?
- 谁？ — Who?
- 什么时候？ — When?
- 哪里？ — Where?
- 怎么样？ — How is it?
- 喜欢吗？ — Do you like it?
- 跟谁一起？ — With whom?
- 什么时候开始？ — When did it start?
- 多久一次？ — How often?
- 常这样吗？ — Is it often like this?
- 真的吗？ — Really?
- 为什么这样？ — Why is it like this?
- 谁告诉你的？ — Who told you?
- 你怎么知道？ — How do you know?
- 难不难？ — Is it difficult?
- 有意思吗？ — Is it interesting?
- 你觉得怎么样？ — What do you think?
- 然后呢？ — And then?
- 后来呢？ — What happened later?
- 现在呢？ — What about now?
- 以前呢？ — What about before?
- 还有吗？ — Anything else?
- 你喜欢哪个？ — Which do you like?
- 哪个更好？ — Which is better?
- 为什么喜欢？ — Why do you like it?

Design rule:
Use 2–3 of these loops after any core question to keep the conversation alive.
