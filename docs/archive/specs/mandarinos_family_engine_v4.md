
# MandarinOS Conversation Engine
# Family Engine v4

Purpose:
Talk about family structure, relationships, and where family members live.

Family conversations reveal:
- siblings
- parents
- marriage / partner
- children
- grandparents
- where family lives
- living arrangements

These become strong conversational memory anchors.

---

# Core Questions (P1)

[?] 你家有几个人？
nǐ jiā yǒu jǐ gè rén
How many people are in your family?

[?] 你有兄弟姐妹吗？
nǐ yǒu xiōngdì jiěmèi ma
Do you have siblings?

[?] 你结婚了吗？
nǐ jiéhūn le ma
Are you married?

[?] 你有孩子吗？
nǐ yǒu háizi ma
Do you have children?

# Only-Child Branch

Trigger:
我没有兄弟姐妹。
I don't have siblings.

[T] 你是独生子女吗？
Are you an only child?

[T] 你的父母在哪儿？
Where are your parents?

[L] 你常回家吗？
Do you often go home?

[L] 你多久回一次家？
How often do you go home?

---

# Parent Living Branch

[T] 你跟父母住吗？
Do you live with your parents?

Example answers:

我跟父母住。 — I live with my parents.  
我自己住。 — I live by myself.  
我跟朋友住。 — I live with friends.

[L] 他们住得远吗？
Do they live far away?

[L] 你多久回家一次？
How often do you go home?

[L] 你喜欢自己住吗？
Do you like living by yourself?

# P2 Conversation Expansion

[L] 你跟谁最像？
Who are you most like?

[L] 你更像爸爸还是妈妈？
Are you more like your father or your mother?

[L] 他们忙吗？
Are they busy?

[L] 他们喜欢什么？

[T] 你家在城市还是农村？
nǐ jiā zài chéngshì háishì nóngcūn
Is your family in the city or countryside?

# Bridges

[B→Place] 他们住在哪儿？
[B→Study/Work] 她做什么？
[B→Identity] 她叫什么名字？

Example:
我有一个哥哥，叫张伟。
I have an older brother called Zhang Wei.

# Example Conversation

你结婚了吗？
Are you married?

结婚了。
Yes.

你爱人做什么？
What does your partner do?

她是老师。
She is a teacher.

你们住在哪儿？
Where do you live?

我们住在上海。
We live in Shanghai.
