# MandarinOS Emergency Phrases (P1 + P2)

Purpose: Help learners repair conversations when they do not understand,
need time, or need to shift to an easier topic.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## P1 Emergency Layer (Survival)

### Level 1 --- Clarification

**什么？**\
shénme\
What?

Use: when you did not catch what the other person said.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Level 2 --- Repeat

**再说一次**\
zài shuō yí cì\
Say it again

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Level 3 --- Slow down

**慢一点**\
màn yì diǎn\
Slower

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Level 4 --- I don't understand

**我不懂**\
wǒ bù dǒng\
I don't understand

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Level 5 --- What does it mean?

**什么意思？**\
shénme yìsi\
What does it mean?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Level 6 --- Buy time

**等一下**\
děng yí xià\
Wait a moment

**我想想**\
wǒ xiǎng xiǎng\
Let me think

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Level 7 --- I don't know

**不知道**\
bù zhī dào\
I don't know

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Level 8 --- Topic reset

**听不懂**\
tīng bù dǒng\
I can't understand

Use: when the conversation has gone beyond your current level and needs
to shift to an easier topic.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## P2 Emergency Layer (Conversation Repair)

**不好意思，我没听懂。**\
bù hǎo yìsi, wǒ méi tīng dǒng\
Sorry, I didn't understand.

**你可以再说一次吗？**\
nǐ kěyǐ zài shuō yí cì ma\
Can you say that again?

**你可以说慢一点吗？**\
nǐ kěyǐ shuō màn yìdiǎn ma\
Can you speak more slowly?

**这个词是什么意思？**\
zhège cí shì shénme yìsi\
What does this word mean?

**让我想一想。**\
ràng wǒ xiǎng yì xiǎng\
Let me think.

**我们聊点简单的吧。**\
wǒmen liáo diǎn jiǎndān de ba\
Let's talk about something easier.

**我们可以聊别的吗？**\
wǒmen kěyǐ liáo bié de ma\
Can we talk about something else?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Repair Ladder

1.  什么？ → repeat\
2.  再说一次 → repeat clearly\
3.  慢一点 → slower\
4.  我不懂 → simplify\
5.  什么意思？ → explain\
6.  等一下 / 我想想 → pause\
7.  听不懂 → change topic

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Notes

-   **什么？** is the most natural first repair move in spoken Chinese.
-   P1 should stay extremely short and usable under pressure.
-   P2 adds politeness and fuller repair language.
-   Topic reset should smoothly downgrade to an easier engine rather
    than stopping the conversation.
