
# MandarinOS Interests Engine v1 (Locked Draft)

Purpose:
Explore what people enjoy doing in their free time, including both modern hobbies and traditional Chinese cultural interests.

This engine helps learners talk about:
- hobbies
- lifestyle
- personal interests
- traditional cultural activities
- collecting
- recommendations
- cultural pride

The engine is designed with:
- P1 simple spoken Chinese
- P2 curiosity expansion
- strong bridges to Travel, Food, Family, Work, and Culture

--------------------------------------------------
P1 ENTRY QUESTIONS

你喜欢做什么？
What do you like to do?

你平时喜欢做什么？
What do you like to do in your free time?

Alternative:
你有什么爱好？
What hobbies do you have?

--------------------------------------------------
P1 CORE FOLLOW-UPS

你常做吗？
Do you do it often?

难吗？
Is it difficult?

你在哪里做？
Where do you do it?

你跟谁一起做？
Who do you do it with?

你推荐吗？
Do you recommend it?

为什么？
Why?

--------------------------------------------------
STRONG BRIDGES

Travel bridge:
你去哪里做这个？
Where do you go to do this?

Example:
我喜欢爬山
I like hiking

你去哪里爬山？
Where do you go hiking?

Food bridge:
你会做什么菜？
What dishes can you cook?

Family bridge:
你跟谁一起做？
Who do you do it with?

Work bridge:
有人把这个变成工作，你觉得怎么样？
Some people turn this into a job — what do you think?

Culture bridge:
你喜欢中国音乐还是外国音乐？
Do you prefer Chinese music or foreign music?

--------------------------------------------------
CULTURAL INTERESTS MODULE

Purpose:
Open up conversations about Chinese traditional activities and cultural hobbies.

P1 cultural entry:
你喜欢中国文化吗？
Do you like Chinese culture?

你喜欢什么？
What do you like?

Example answers:
我喜欢书法
I like calligraphy

我喜欢太极
I like tai chi

我喜欢喝茶
I like drinking tea

CULTURAL CURIOSITY PATTERN

These questions work across almost any cultural topic.

难吗？
Is it difficult?

很多人学吗？
Do many people learn it?

你怎么开始的？
How did you start?

你学了多久？
How long have you studied it?

你学得好吗？
Are you good at it?

这是中国传统吗？
Is this a Chinese tradition?

年轻人喜欢吗？
Do young people like it?

外国人学吗？
Do foreigners learn it?

你推荐吗？
Do you recommend it?

为什么推荐？
Why do you recommend it?

你觉得中国文化怎么样？
What do you think about Chinese culture?

COLLECTING BRANCH

你收藏什么吗？
Do you collect anything?

Examples:
茶 — tea
玉 — jade
邮票 — stamps
古董 — antiques

Follow-up:
为什么喜欢收藏？
Why do you like collecting?

--------------------------------------------------
WHY THIS ENGINE MATTERS

The Interests engine reveals personality.

It includes:
- modern hobbies
- traditional cultural interests
- collecting
- recommendation loops
- strong bridges to other engines

This makes it one of the richest conversation engines in MandarinOS.
